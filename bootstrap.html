Personal Profile

About ME Simms:

I am a former education manager for science centers and museums. For years, I mentored informal science educators on communication techniques that removed the ambiguity around complex contepts.

Instead, we blew people's minds. We sparked the joy of discovery. We created awe for our strange universe.

I have used these same communication strategies to create new marketing campaigns, learning resources, and training modules to help our science educations reach more students. I have increased program sales for a variety of organizations, both non-profit and corporate. We used data-driven decision making to improve our programs and our communication efforts. We used collaborative teamwork techniques to bring the best out of everyone and every project.

This approach focuses on the belief that everyone is a valuable collaborator. Each person can contribute to success for the entire team, while personally growing through the shared experience. I now bring the power of online tools to this approach to help your audience succeed.
