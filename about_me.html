Hire for Character


Skills can be taught. Knowledge can be learned.

Character & Attitude impacts your team's culture.

It's the people who drive you towards your mission.


We believe the world needs curious, forward-thinking, solutions-oriented people who want to make our planet better.

Create a culture focused on open communication, mutual respect at all levels, promoting innovation, and embracing creative ideas from everyone.


In the early 2000's, Social Scientists discovered the 24 character strengths that are universal across all societies in the world. Every individual possess all 24 character strengths. But each individual has a unique profile based on the degrees that these strengths manifest themselves. The VIA Institute on Character can identify a persons top 5 character strengths.

Understanding a person's strength helps you understand how they might support your team culture.

Michael's Top Five Character Strengths

Leadership

Leadership

Virtue: Justice

Encouraging a group to get things done and at the same time maintain good relations within the group; organizing group activities and seeing that they happen.

Creativity

Creativity

Virtue: Wisdom

Thinking of novel and productive ways to conceptualize and do things; includes artistic achievement but is not limited to it.

Fairness

Fairness

Virtue: Justice

Treating all people the same according to notions of fairness and justice; not letting feelings bias decisions about others; giving everyone a fair chance.

Judgement

Judgement

Virtue: Wisdom

Thinking things through and examining them from all sides; not jumping to conclusions; being able to change one's mind in light of evidence; weighing all evidence fairly.

Perspective

Perspective

Virtue: Wisdom

Being able to provide wise counsel to others; having ways of looking at the world that make sense to oneself/others.


People perform best when they focus on using their top strengths.

Our greatest opportunities for growth, development, and ultimately success lie in building on our strengths.
